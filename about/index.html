About Us

Welcome to PotaTOE Craft, a family-friendly Minecraft server where fun and safety come first!
Hi, I’m Ben! I’m 11 years old, and I’ve always been fascinated by technology.
My dad works in cybersecurity (making tech as secure as possible),
so I’ve grown up with a deep appreciation for creating online spaces that are safe, secure, and fun for everyone.
PotaTOE Craft is my passion project: a kid-friendly Minecraft server designed for kid gamers to enjoy without worrying about online drama or unsafe environments.

Why Choose PotaTOE Craft?

At PotaTOE Craft, we believe Minecraft should be a space where kids can explore their creativity,
learn new skills, and connect with others in a safe and positive environment.
To make this possible, we’ve carefully selected plugins that enhance gameplay while keeping the server secure and easy to use.

Kid-Friendly and Parent-Approved

PotaTOE Craft is designed with families in mind.
I know how important it is for parents to feel confident about where their kids are spending time online, so I’ve worked hard to create a safe, welcoming environment.
Whether your child loves building, exploring, or just playing with friends, PotaTOE Craft offers a secure and fun experience. And thanks to plugins like CoreProtect and EssentialsXChat, I can ensure that players are always respectful and the community stays in good shape.
